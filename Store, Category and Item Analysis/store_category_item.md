# Store, Category and Item Analysis

## Introduction
This analysis is meant to unearth **dominant stores, categories and items** that topped the charts in **Iowa** from **2016-2025**. 

![Liquor Store](https://github.com/AdenKabz/Iowa_Liquor_Procurement/blob/main/images/Iowa%20liquor%20stores.jpeg)

A Liquor Store in Iowa <br><br>

<ins>Core Question:</ins> *What are the dominant stores, categories and items in the procurement space?* <br><br>

## Dominant Stores
The figure below shows the **top 10 stores** that prevailed across the various procurement metrics within Iowa state:

![Top 10 Stores](https://github.com/AdenKabz/Iowa_Liquor_Procurement/blob/main/images/store%20metrics.png)
[Tableau Link - Store Analysis](https://public.tableau.com/views/IowaLiquorProcurementAnalysis/Store-KeyMetrics?:language=en-GB&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)
- **Hy-Vee #3/ BDI/ Des Moines** and **Central City 2** are the **top 2 stores** leading with **very high procurement numbers** in terms of **total transactions**, **total procurement expenditure**, **total bottles procured**, **total cases procured** and **total volume procured liters**.
- In terms of **average price per liter**, each of the **top 10 stores** has an **average price pper liter** above **$60**. In addition, **Hy-Vee Gas #5018/ Ames** has the **very highest average price per liter** that goes way beyond **$240**.

## Dominant Categories
The figure below indicates the **top 10 categories** that have earned a crown across the different procurement metrics in Iowa:

![Top 10 Categories](https://github.com/AdenKabz/Iowa_Liquor_Procurement/blob/main/images/category%20metrics.png)
[Tableau Link - Category Analysis](https://public.tableau.com/views/IowaLiquorProcurementAnalysis/Category-KeyMetrics?:language=en-GB&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)
- **American Vodkas** consistently dominates with **very high procurement figures** in terms of **total transactions**, **total procurement expenditure**, **total bottles procured**, **total cases procured** and **total volume procured liters**.
- The **average price per liter** is above **$40** for the **top 10 categories**. Additionally, **Imported Whiskies** has the **greatest average price per liter** that exceeds **$120**.

## Dominant Items 
The figure below shows the **top 10 items** that reigned across the various procurement metrics in Iowa:

![Top 10 Items](https://github.com/AdenKabz/Iowa_Liquor_Procurement/blob/main/images/item%20metrics.png)
[Tableau Link - Item Analysis](https://public.tableau.com/views/IowaLiquorProcurementAnalysis/Item-KeyMetrics?:language=en-GB&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link)
- **Black Velvet**, **Titos Handmade Vodka**, **Hawkeye Vodka** and **Fireball Cinnamon Whiskey** are consistently the **top 4 items** in terms of **total transactions**, **total cases procured** and **total volume procured liters** with **Black Velvet** being on the very top.
- In terms of **total procurement expenditure**, **Titos Handmade Vodka** leads with very high numbers followed by **Black Velvet** and **Fireball Cinnamon Whiskey**.
- In relation to **total bottles procured**, **Fireball Cinnamon Whiskey** has the very highest figures followed by **Black Velvet**, **Titos Handmade Vodka** and **Hawkeye Vodka**.
- The **average price per liter** for the **top 10 items** exceeds **$5500** implying these are **ultra-premium** alcoholic beverages. Conversely, the very top item here, **Ha Midleton Silent Distillery Chapter 4** has a crazy **average price per liter** that is above **$50,000**. <br><br>
